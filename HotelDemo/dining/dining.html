Dining

More Travel Guide Four-Star Award-winning restaurants than any other resort in Europe. The first Chinese restaurant in the country to be awarded a Michelin Star and the only Forbes Travel Guide Five-Star Award-winning Chinese restaurant in Europe. Fish caught in Hawaiian waters that arrive fresher to your table in Verona than they do in Maui’s own restaurants. One of four restaurants in the world to serve a beef even more rare than certified Kobe (but we serve that, too). These are the superlatives that make dining at Verona Hotel and Encore so exciting.
